Hello, I'm Toby.

Intro Bio Blog

I made this site during my gap year before college in 2017. After a bit of a retirement, I'm bringing it back to showcase some of the side projects I've worked on over the last few years.

I grew up in rural Putney, Vermont. I like skiing, observing insects and other wildlife, jumping into water, and board games.

I graduated from Middlebury College with a degree in math, focusing on the mathematical sciences, in May 2022. I also took a lot of physics, CS, and history-ish classes, and was the copresident of the Middlebury Snow Bowl ski patrol.

I'm currently working as a researcher on a contract with fp21.
